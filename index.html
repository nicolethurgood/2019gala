Class of 2019 Gala

Presented by RIT College Activities Board

Sold Out

about

Come out and enjoy a formal night to celebrate your accomplishments with your fellow classmates. Create a few last memories over food, drinks, and entertainment!

When

November 30th, 2018

7:00pm to 11:00pm

Where

The Wintergarden by Monroe's

One Bausch & Lomb Place

Rochester, NY 14604

Tickets

Tickets are $15 for RIT seniors and guests

Dress Code

This is a formal event, so dress your best!

Requirements

Bring both state issued ID or passport and school ID.

Guests

A guest can be someone that does not attend RIT or be an RIT student that is not yet a 4th year.

Transportation

Buses are provided to and from the Wintergarden from 6:30pm - 11:00pm. Parking is not provided.

Dinner

Served from 7:00pm - 9:00pm, and a cash only bar will be available all night.

Casino

Head upstairs to enjoy casino games where you can win great prizes!
